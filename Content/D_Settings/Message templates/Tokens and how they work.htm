Tokens and how they work

HTML tokens are the most useful asset to Message Templates. They are used when Message Templates need to apply to specific information or general groups. For example, the template Discussion – InappropriateBehaviour is generated when people report an inappropriate comment or post in Discussion Forums.

This will appear like:

- Person reporting inappropriate behaviour – John Doe

- Email address of person reporting inappropriate behaviour – Jdoe@email.com.au

- Person being reported – Jane Smith (jsmith@email.com.au)
