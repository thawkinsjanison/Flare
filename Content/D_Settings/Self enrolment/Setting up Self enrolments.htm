Setting up Self enrolments
  • Navigate to the Settings menu.
  • Find and select Self Enrolment Settings.

  • You need to set your Default Enrolment Method. If you have not specified which enrolment rule at a catalogue item level this rule will be the default.
  • Options for this list include:
  • Self-enrolment (Free)
  • Self-enrolment (Paid)
  • No approval
  • No approval, enrol and launch immediately
  • 1 – Step approval (Manager)
  • 1 – Step approval (Administrator)
  • 2 – Step approval (Manager > Manager)
  • 2 – Step approval (Manager > Administrator)

These options are detailed more below.
