The Self Enrolment Wizard

The Self Enrolment Wizard will assist users in securing their payment for the course. To test this find a Course you would like to enrol in, if the Course needs to be paid for you will be prompted with the price.

Select Enrol.

  • The wizard will display an overview of the course you are paying for, how much the course costs and allow you to choose your payment method.

  • Select the PayPal payment method.
  • Choose Next and select Pay with my PayPal account.
  • Login to PayPal and review your purchase information the same as you would while shopping online.
  • Select Pay Now.
  • You will be re-directed to your Janison CLS site.
  • Select Finish to exit the Self Enrolment Wizard.
  • The user is now enrolled and has paid for the Course using PayPal.
