Skin

A Skin specifies the values for text, backgrounds and accents on the CLS screens. To change the skin select Settings > Style and Images and click the Skin tab.

You can select a pre-defined skin from the drop-down box:

Each time you click a skin name, the screen display updates, so that you can get an idea of its palette.

To create your own skin, change the colours for the options presented on the screen. The technique is the same for each option:

  1. Click the arrow next to the name of the option.
  2. Paste a new colour value (in hexadecimal notation), or
  3. Drag the circle in the colour picker to select the level of saturation and brightness of the colour.
  4. Drag the slider to change the hue of the colour, if desired.
  5. Click Apply

The screen will show the results of your choices for: Header and Footer text colours, ordinary text, links and the accent colour.

Click Save Skin to save your changes.

When choosing colours the best way to identify all the places the colour changes take effect is to set them to a colour that stands out and click through a few pages. When you understand the change you can set them to the required colour.

If the top and bottom of the header are different, the header will have a colour gradient. The same principle applies to the footer.
