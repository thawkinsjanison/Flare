Roles Manager – List Form

The List of Roles is located under Settings > Roles.

On this page you can:

  1. Search for Roles.
  2. View the categories of Roles, All, Main Role, Extra Role, Inactive and Implicit.
  3. It shows a list of available roles in the system. These can be sorted alphabetically by clicking the icon.
  4. The Role descriptions outlining their purpose.
  5. A link exists to all users in the system who are members of the role.
