Setting up management relationships

Management relationships in the CLS can be run in different ways, as discussed in the introduction:

  1. Position-to-Position manager relationships

    2. Positions are used explicitly for the management hierarchy and the Position function is visible in the Manage People menu.

  1. User-to-User manager relationships

    2. Here the management hierarchy is present in the system, but less visible. The Position function is not present in the Manage People left hand menu.

  1. Not at all: Positions are disabled within the system.

    2. There is no Relationship Management or Position functionality at all. The rest of this document is not relevant if option 3 is chosen.

To set up Positions go to Settings > Tenant Settings.

Click the chevron next to Organisation and then click in the drop-down box next to Relationship Management Type. Click and select an option:

  1. Positions for Position-to-Position management
  2. Report to User for User-to-User management.
  3. None

Click Save Settings.
