Inspect a Position Details

To look at the a Position details screen, navigate to Manage People > Positions and click the name of the Position.

This gives you access to information about:

  1. the Organisation Unit it is attached to.
  2. the number of Users who have the Position. This is a hyperlink to a list of these Users.
  3. who the Position reports to (if set), with hyperlinks to that Position and the User(s) who hold it.
  4. who reports to the Position, both directly and indirectly: hyperlinks to the list of Users and to the Position(s).
