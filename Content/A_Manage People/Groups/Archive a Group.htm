Archive a Group

The Archive feature removes the Group item from the list of active group items.  This assists in keeping the list to a minimum. The archived item can be accessed from the Inactive tab.  Generally, archived items are items no longer required.

Navigate to Manage People > Groups.

  1. Groups can be archived by checking the box to the left of the group name and clicking Archive.

  1. Groups can be un-archived by selecting the Inactive tab, checking the box beside the group you wish to retrieve and clicking Unarchive.
