Organisation unit Introduction

Organisation Units provide further division within an organisation structure.  For example schools within a school system, or departments within a hospital.  An Organisation Unit must belong to exactly one Organisation.  This feature is used by high level administrators only. 

Organisation Units can be accessed by selecting Manage People > Organisation Units.

There are two methods to creating an Organisation Unit within an Organisation:

  1. Manually adding an Organisation Unit.
  2. Importing from a CSV.
