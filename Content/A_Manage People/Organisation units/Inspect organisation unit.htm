Inspect organisation unit

Inspecting an Organisation Unit gives you access to information about which organisation the unit belongs to, number of users, positions and groups, as well as when the Organisation Unit was last updated.

To inspect an Organisation Unit navigate to Manage People > Organisation Units.

  1. Search for Organisation Unit using the Search menu.  Or
  2. Click the Organisation Unit from the Display Name list as highlighted by the arrow in the screenshot below.

  1. Inspect details as required.
