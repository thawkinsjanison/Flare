Inspect group types

Inspecting a Group Type's details gives you access to information on the Name of Group, Group Id, as well as links to a list of the Groups that are members of this Group Type.

To inspect a Group Type navigate to Manage People > Group Types.

  1. Search for Group Type using the Search menu. Or
  2. Click on the Group Type from the Display Name list as highlighted by the arrow in the screenshot.

  1. Inspect details as required.
