Archive organisation

Organisations should be archived rather than deleted. There are two ways to archive an organisation:

  1. Archive via Edit Organisation.
  2. Archive via Organisation List.

Archive via Edit Organisation

  • Search for the Organisation using the search menu.  Or
  • Click on the Organisation from the Display Name list.

    1. Check the box beside Inactive.
    2. Click Save Organisation.

    Archive via Organisation List

    Select Manage People > Organisations.

    1. Locate the name of the Organisation you want to archive in the list of Organisations and click the check box to its left.
    2. ClickArchive.
    3. This Organisation will now be listed as an inactive Organisation. You can see the list by clicking its tab. You can reverse your archive decision by using theUnarchivebutton at the bottom of the Inactive list.
