Account Activation / Deactivation

Individual accounts or groups of accounts can be set to active or inactive using the Deactivate Accounts or Activate Accounts features.

  1. Select Deactivate Accounts or Activate Accounts.
  2. Enter a Description. This is to identify the bulk action in case there are many being created.
  3. Specify the filters to target the appropriate users. Refer to Building Filters for more information.
    There will be a note on how many users will be affected by the action.
  4. Enter your Password.
  5. Select Deactivate Accounts or Activate Accounts.
    A confirmation popup will be displayed.

  1. Select Yes.

Once the action is performed, you will be directed to the Scheduled Job Details page with information on the status of the action.  Further details can be seen when selecting the Details link under Results.
