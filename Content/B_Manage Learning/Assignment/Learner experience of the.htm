Learner experience of the Assignment

The Learner will access the assignment the same way that they will access any other learning asset. This is usually by browsing to My Learning and launching the assignment from the tile.

The experience will be slightly different depending on the configuration of the assignment as covered in the previous section. In this example the assignment is configured as below.

  1. On first visit the Status will be Not Submitted. This will change as the Learner progresses through the assignment.
  2. The Learner can enter text as well as upload files to the post.
  3. If the option to manually assign competencies is checked as covered in the previous section, the Learner will need to select the competencies they are applying for with the post.
  4. The Learner can make one or more posts.
  5. When ready the Learner can browse to the Actions dropdown and send the assignment to the marker For Review or Make a Submission. Either of the submission types will lock the assignment from further posts.
