Assignment Competencies

Competencies and Competency Sets can be linked to the Assignment by selecting the Competencies collapsible window and following the steps to link Competences.

There is an extra option with Assignments, which is the way they are awarded to a learner. Select either:

  • automatically award all Competencies or Competency Sets when learners pass the Assignment, or
  • Marker selects the Competencies or Competency Sets that the learner demonstrates during the Assignment
