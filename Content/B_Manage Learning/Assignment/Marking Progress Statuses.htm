Marking Progress Status

The status can have a number of values.

Require Resubmission will unlock the attempt for the Learner so that they are able to make changes.

Mark Assignment Submission will open options for marking, as seen below. It will put the Assignment in a Status of Marking.

  1. If the Score entered here is less than the Passmark the Learner will fail the Assignment. To pass the Learner a mark equal to or greater than the Passmark must be entered.
  2. The Comment field is private and anything entered here will not be available to the Learner.
  3. The Competency or Competencies awarded to the Learner for the submission must be selected here. If the Assignment is set to award Competencies automatically these values will not be editable.
  4. Save Score will not finalise the marking and further edits will be possible.
  5. Finalise will complete the marking and change the status to Pass or Fail. A confirmation pop-up will give the marker the option to continue or cancel marking.
