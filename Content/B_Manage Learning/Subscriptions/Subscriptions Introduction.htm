Subscriptions – Introduction

Subscriptions allow you to manage access to catalogue items in a way that matches your business model. You can set up various subscriptions with different rates. Some examples include:

  • A 10% discount could apply to items for a 1 month subscription.
  • A 50% discount could apply to items for a 12 month subscription.
  • A bronze level membership could give you a 15% discount for the year.
  • A platinum membership could give you a 100% discount for the year.
  • A lifetime subscription could give you an 80% discount for life.
  • A ‘Technology Pack’ subscription could give you a 70% discount on all items in the ‘Technology Pack’.
  • A negative rate subscription increases the price of some items. This could then be combined with a discount subscription.
