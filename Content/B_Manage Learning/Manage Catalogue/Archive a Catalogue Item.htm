Archive a Catalogue Item

The archive feature removes a Catalogue Item from the list of active Catalogue Items. This means that Users can no longer enrol or be enrolled in it. The archived item can be accessed from the Archived tab. It is recommended that no Catalogue Item be deleted (through Edit Catalogue Item) but instead archived.

To archive a Catalogue Item:

  • Select Manage Learning >Manage Catalogue
  • Click the checkbox next to the item
  • Click Archive

Note that you can change the Status of an archived Catalogue Item in the same screen

  • Click the checkbox next to the item
  • Click Make Private or Make Public

You can also make this change of Status when you inspect the Catalogue Item (see Inspect Catalogue Item). The Status can be changed to Publish as public (available in catalogue) or Publish as private (not available in catalogue).
