Make a Catalogue Item private or public

The Make Private feature hides Catalogue Item(s) from learners so that they canâ€™t see them in Browse Learning or self-enrol in them (if that was enabled). However authorised Users can still enrol learners in private Catalogue Items.

The Make Public feature is the opposite. Thus a public Catalogue Item is visible to learners in Browse Learning and they can self-enrol (if this had been enabled).

To make a Catalogue Item private:

  • Select Manage Learning >Manage Catalogue
  • Click the Available in Catalogue tag
  • Click the checkbox next to the Item
  • Click Make Private

To make a Catalogue Item public:

  • Select Manage Learning >Manage Catalogue
  • Click the Not in Catalogue tag
  • Click the checkbox next to the Item
  • Click Make Public
