Completion Rules

This is how you determine how your learner can complete their learning plan and, once they do, what happens next.

The on-screen instructions will tell you what each option means.

Rollover – this allows the learner to take incomplete learning plan entries from a plan they are completing into their new plan. When a leaner rolls a plan over, a new enrolment in the same plan is automatically created and incomplete learning plan entries are populated to it.

Can edit after completion – allows a learner to edit a plan, even if they’ve completed it.

Be careful! If the user rolls their plan over, they are creating a new enrolment in the same learning plan. They can only have one current enrolment in a learning plan so, once the new one is created and ready to edit, they will no longer be allowed to edit their old plans even if you check this option.
