Inspect Learning Event details

You can inspect a Learning Event from Manage Sessions by clicking the name of a linked event, which is also a hyperlink.

You can also do this from the Manage a Session and Mark a Session screens where the linked event is listed at the top of the screen.

You are then taken to the Manage Learning Assets section of the CLS to inspect the Learning Event's details. The page also provides links enabling you to add Sessions to the Learning Event or edit it.

On the details screen are:

  1. The main characteristics of the Learning Event, including options chosen when it was created and whether it is active.
  2. The number of attendees in each of the status categories: Waitlisted, Attending, Attended, Interviewed or RPL, Did not attend.
  3. The number of people on Waitlists, displayed prominently.

Sessions

Click Learning Event Sessions to open the window and view its Sessions.

  1. The triangle icon next to the headings can be used to filter the list, e.g. restricting it to one Location.

  1. Each Session can be viewed, edited, managed or marked by clicking the appropriate icon at its right hand side. Click the eye to inspect, the pencil to edit, the people to manage and the book and tick to mark.
