Linking Discussion Forums to Module Pages

Adding a module page identifier to the forum in the Linked Document section will add a forum widget to the page in the course player.

A similar feature is available for Custom Pages. Custom pages can be made available to Users outside of the course player, for example pinned to the Home page. See the document on Custom Pages for further information on how to do this.

  • Browse to Design > Module. Open the page of the module you would like to forum linked to.
  • Copy the identifier of the page as shown in the screenshot below.

  • Open the Discussion Forum in edit mode.
  • Enter the identifier of the module page in the Linked Documents field.
  • Click Add.
  • Save the Discussion Forum.

The forum widget will appear on the right hand side of the course as shown below and it will be possible for the Learner to make posts directly from the course.
