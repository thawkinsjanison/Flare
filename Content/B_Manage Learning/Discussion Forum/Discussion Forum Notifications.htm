Discussion Forum - Notifications

Discussion Forum Notifications (DFNs) give users the option to receive email notifications of all or specific posts and comments in Discussion Forums.

The following 2 settings are required for notifications to work

  • Per-user emails are enabled by the administrator by browsing to Settings > Email. Select Per-user notifications.

  • Enable Notifications in the Discussion Forum form is checked

 

Participant Notification Options

Several notification options are available by opening the forum and browsing to Notifications.

  1. All forum posts and comments.  The participant will receive notifications for all posts and comments.
  2. All posts to this forum.  The participant will receive notifications for all posts to the forum but not comments
  3. Posts from people I’m following.  The participant will receive notifications for posts from people they are following.
  4. All Comments to this forum.  The participant will receive notifications for all comments to the posts but not posts.
  5. Comments by people I'm following.  The participant will receive notifications for comments of people they are following.
  6. Comments to posts I'm following.  The participant will receive notifications for comments to posts they are following.
  7. Comments on my posts.  The participant will receive notifications for comments on posts they have made.
