Discussion Forum - Introduction

Discussion forums are a fantastic way for users to communicate with each other and receive feedback on comments from peers and facilitators.   Participants can make posts to a forum and also comment on posts.

Some of the features of the Janison Discussion Forums are:

  • The ability to Follow posts.
  • The ability to set participants as Favourites
  • To set email Notifications for only the updates of interest for you.
  • Attach files to posts.
  • Categorise posts by their nature such as General posts, Questions, Support for the discussion topic or Challenge of the topic.
  • To set Administrators with additional permissions such as the option to remove posts.
