Notifications

Discussion Forum notifications give users the option to receive email notifications of all or specific posts and/or comments.

Two settings are required for notifications to work:

  • Per-user notifications is enabled in Settings.
  • Enable Notifications is enabled in the forum.

To enable Per-user notifications browse to Settings > Email Settings

  1. Select Per-user notifications.

Navigate back to your forum and open it in edit mode.

  1. In the Settings section select Enable Notifications.

User notification options

Several notification options are available by opening the forum.

  1. Select Notifications from the bottom right of the screen.

  1. Select the types of messages you would like to receive notifications for.
  2. Select Save.
