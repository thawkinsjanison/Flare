Discussion Forum - Administrators

An additional role can be assigned to Discussion Forum participants which will enhance their permissions to edit and hide posts.  In the Discussion Forum Administrators section search for the participant you require to facilitate the Discussion Forum, select the Role and Add.  If the Role dropdown is empty please see the next section of this document.

Making a role available in the Role dropdown.

This is a one off setting that needs to be made to make the facilitator role appear in the Role dropdown of the Discussion Forum Administrators section.  Without setting this the Role dropdown may be empty.

Browse to Settings > Roles and open the role Facilitator.

In the Applicable Documents section check Discussion Forum as shown below.

Administrators will appear in the right hand column of the Discussion Forum.  They will have an Edit Pencil to make changes to all posts in the forum.  Hiding a post by clicking the eye will hide it from all participants.  A participant will also see these icons but they will only be able to make edits to their own posts and hiding a post will only hide it from themselves, not all participants.
