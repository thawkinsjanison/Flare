2 Setup – Enabling Endorsements

There is a second part to setting up a competency which requires Enabling Endorsements. Endorsements is what will allow you to link your competency to activities. Activities are effectively just pages inside a module but they are what will allow your learners to achieve the badge.

  1. Under the Settings menu select the item titled Competency Settings
  2. You will need to Enable Endorsements in order to link your activities. There are extra options on this page to:
  1. Add FAQs and an FAQ URL for your Competency Endorsement page
  1. The FAQ content will appear as a pop-up when selected:

  1. The FAQ URL is intended for custom page use
  1. Issue a badge notification email – the email address you choose will get sent an email saying the person who is receiving credit and for what competency. This is governed by a Message Template and can be altered to fit your organisation or business.

  1. Now that you have enabled endorsements, return to the competency you previously created to link your activities (Manage Learning > Manage Competencies)
  2. You will now see there is an extra option on your competency titled Endorsement Settings

  1. To get started, click Enable Endorsement
