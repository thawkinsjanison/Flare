2 Workflow – Endorsing other Learners

  1. After a learner has applied for endorsement, the Endorse link will light up on other users Endorsements page.

 

  1. After selecting this they will be prompted to either endorse the user or not give them endorsement yet – and give them feedback regarding either one.

  1. Give Endorsement – if they give their ‘peer’ endorsement, this will show up on the peers endorsements page

  1. Not Yet – keeps the other learners on the list to be endorsed but pushes them to the back of the list
    Note: Users can only endorse one peer at a time
