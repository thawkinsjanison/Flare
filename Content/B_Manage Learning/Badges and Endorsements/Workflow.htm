1 Workflow – Completing Linked Activities and Applying for Endorsement

To be able to apply for endorsement a learner must first complete any linked activities to the competency. Since the linked activities are pages (in a module) you’ll want to make sure the learner is enrolled in the course catalogue before the learner can start to achieve endorsement.

After the learner has completed the activity the Evidence submitted block icon will change to a tick, an Apply button will also appear on the right hand side.

When the learner clicks on the apply button – it will confirm they have applied for endorsement.
