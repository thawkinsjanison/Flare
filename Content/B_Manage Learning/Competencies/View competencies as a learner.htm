User experience of Competencies

Competencies achieved can be visible both to learners and to their managers.

View Competencies as a learner

To view their Competencies a learner selects My Account and follows the steps below.

  1. Select the Competency collapsible window to open it.

    2. Three tabs appear: Required, Enrolled and Achieved.

  2. Required lists the Competencies that are required through Competency Requirements(s).

    3. If a Competency has been acquired the Date Completed and Expiry Dates are shown and the Status icon is a tick.

    If it has not been completed, these dates are blank and the Status icon is red with a crossbar

  3. Enrolled lists Competencies linked to Catalogue Items the learner is or has enrolled in and their completion data.
  4. Achieved lists Competencies linked to Catalogue Items the learner has completed and their completion data.

View Competencies as a manager

Under specific circumstances a User can see the Competency Requirement status of their staff on their My Learning screen.

To enable this:

  • The User must be established in the system as a direct manager of these staff through a Position to Position management relationship.
  • The Smart Tile setting must be checked at global level for the tenant.

When these are set, a Competency Requirement tile appears at the bottom of the screen:

  1. Compliance data for all staff directly and indirectly reporting to the User is summarised on the tile.
  2. My Staff Competency Requirements is a link to the Manage Compliance with Competency screen where the data is displayed in detail.

Note:

If you have set this up correctly and do not see the tile, contact Support for help.
