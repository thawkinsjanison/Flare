Step 1 Content

Display the Catalogue Items that you want to enrol Users by:

  • Clicking Search for content or tags and entering search parameters, or
  • Clicking Search

Note: Content can also be categorised by assigning Tags to the Catalogue Items. If Tag items are displayed and selected, the wizard can display Catalogue Items associated with the Tag.

  • Click a Catalogue Item and then click the plus icon at its right to add it. It will then be listed under Added Content.
  • Repeat and add items as required.
  • You can delete an item by clicking the minus icon next to it.
