Step 4 Review/Publish

You may recall that Users cannot actually enrol in a Learning Asset until it is linked to a Catalogue Item. Thus, after Steps 2-3 the Add Learning wizard has created a new Learning Asset and also a new, linked Catalogue Item with the same name as the Learning Asset.

The last screen, the Review/Publish screen, offers you the option to publish the Catalogue Item as private or public. Alternatively, you can edit any details before publishing by selecting the Go Back button.

To complete the wizard click Publish and choose one of the following:

  • Selecting Publish as private will create the Learning Asset and Catalogue Item but they will be hidden from Users. Manage Catalogue will show the Catalogue Item as Not in Catalogue.
  • Selecting Publish as public will make the Learning Asset and Catalogue Item visible to Users. Manage Catalogue will show the Catalogue Item as Available in Catalogue.

After you publish, you are taken to the Manage Catalogue section. You are at the inspection screen for your Catalogue Item where you can inspect various values and add others, see Inspect Catalogue Item details.

Note that if you discontinue the wizard before you complete the last step, a Catalogue Item and Learning Asset are both created. You can locate them through Manage Learning > Manage Catalogue where you will find the Catalogue Item listed amongst the items with Draft status. After clicking it you can edit both the item and the asset.
