Step 3 Details for Learning Event

Learning Events are made up of one or more Sessions where a user must actually attend a venue. Creating a Learning Event through the wizard involves creating an event and then creating its Session(s).

  1. Click the chevron next to Learning Event Settings and enter the required information. Full details about creating Learning Events are at Add Learning Event.
  2. Click the chevron next to Who is facilitating this event?
  3. Select a name from the drop-down box and click the plus icon to add it. You can do this multiple times. You remove a name from the list by clicking the red minus icon that is at its right.
  4. Click the chevron next to Your sessions to open the window.
  5. Click Add Session to enter a Session. You can do this multiple times. Full details about creating Sessions are at Add Session.

  1. When the sessions have been added click Next at the bottom of the page
