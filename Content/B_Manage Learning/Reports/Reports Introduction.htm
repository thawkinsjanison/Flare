Reports - Introduction

Reporting is an integral part of all learning especially in the online forum.  Reports can provide a high level overview of information gathered.  The Janison CLS currently has three reporting sections on the Dashboard.

To access Reports select Manage Learning > Reports.

Assessment Completion Report

The assessment completion report will generate a pie chart indicating test attempts status against elected assessment event.

Select Manage Learning > Reports.

  • Click Assessment Completion Report.

  • Fill out the form details.

  • Click Apply.
