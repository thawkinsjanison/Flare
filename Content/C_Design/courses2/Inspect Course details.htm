Inspect Course details

Inspecting a Course gives you access to information about it and links to its Discipline, Modules and Competencies. To inspect a Course navigate to Design > Courses and click the Course name. This takes you to its details screen.

The name of the Course and the Discipline it belongs to (a clickable link) are listed at the top left hand side.

The widget bar is underneath this. Of particular importance are:

  1. Tags – Courses can be tagged as a way of categorising and locating them, see linking Tags for instructions on how to do this.
  2. The next section displays the Name and Identifier of the Course and indicates whether the Course is Active.

  1. The final section contains three expandable windows giving details about the attached Modules, any linked Competencies and Competency sets and a list of Recent Activities.
