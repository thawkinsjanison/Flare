Search for Templates

The search feature allows you to quickly find a Template.

To search for a Template select Design > Templates.

  1. Click Search for Content Templates and a search field will appear.
  2. Enter search word.
  3. Select the scope of search you require, global, local or all.  Global templates are templates designed by Janison.  Local templates are the customised templates designed by Users within your system.
  4. Click Search.
  5. The search results will appear in the Display Name column.
  6. To perform another search click Reset Search.
