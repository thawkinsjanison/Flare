Manage Assessment Modules

Assessment Modules are managed by selecting Design > Modules.

The screen shows all of the Modules in all Disciplines, divided into three categories.

  1. Click the Assessment Modules heading to show the Assessment Modules.
  2. Click the Search bar to search within the Assessment Modules.
    • Enter search word in the search field.
    • Click Search.
    • The search results will appear.
    • To perform another search click Reset Search.
  1. You can order the list of Modules by name or date of last update by clicking the heading names.
