Surveys – introduction

A survey is a very useful tool for gathering information and feedback from a large group of people. There are two ways a survey can be used within the CLS.  These are:

  • Surveys added to a module of a course player – are limited to two question types and are useful for the capture of feedback about specific modules.
  • Surveys added to a survey player – have additional functionality and would be used to gather broader feedback for example, about the overall learning experience of the site.

Surveys can be found by navigating to Design > Tests and Surveys.
