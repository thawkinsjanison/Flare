Janison Academy

Social Learning is a Janison Plugin designed to make learning fun. When active, the CLS becomes known as the Janison Academy.

Learners can sign up and start learning in just 60 seconds.

Social Learning uses features such as Leaderboards and Challenges to provide learning incentives to users. Content can be tailored and filtered using Interests and Recommendations.

If you would like to learn more about social learning visit our Website or contact your Janison Account Manager.
